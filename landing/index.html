Why trust an AI with your degree plan?

Because we don't trust it blindly either. AI models sometimes invent a course or break a rule. So every generated plan is run through a deterministic checker that confirms each module exists in your handbook, isn't one you've already passed, respects prerequisites and take-limits, and that the credits add up. If the AI slips, you see exactly where — you never follow a broken plan.
